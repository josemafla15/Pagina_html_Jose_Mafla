  Pasto  
 
    Pasto is a city in western Colombia known for his black a whites 
    Carnival. The carnival museum houses giant figres used in the 
    celebratory parades. The colonial, moorish-influenced Temple of St.
    John the Baptist sits dbeside central Nariño Square. The Banco de la 
    Republica Gold Musem displays precious pre-Columbian artifacts.

    Southwest of Pasto is Las Lajas Sanctuary, a gothic church built into a 
    gorge.
